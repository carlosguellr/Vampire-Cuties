Vampire Cuties is a time, wave-based game where you are the bullet hell.

In this game you will play the hero, who faces an "endless" amounts of waves of enemies with weapons that you will gain and strengthen by defeating said enemies. The game has a set runtime of 5-minutes, after that the game will automatically end. Try to defeat as many enemies in that time range!

Features:

  • Automated attacking
  • Multiple weapons that each act in their own way! Start with one random weapon, level up to randomly gain the rest!
    • Ring of Onion - Damages enemies that are in range over time
    • Dagger Throw - Throws low damage daggers horizontally across the screen
    • Fire Gauntlet - High damage short range attack in the faced direction
    • Axe Spree - Throws three axes in a random direction dealing high damage
  • Arcade-like gameplay loop (5 minute runtime)
  • Level-up system - weapon and enemy power scaling
  • "Endless map" scroll effect
